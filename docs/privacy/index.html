Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

FarmTracker ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Character Data: Character names, tags, and currency information you create

  • Purchase Data: App store transaction identifiers for Premium feature verification

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers unless you explicitly share it.

4. Data Usage

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide farming session tracking functionality
  • Generate statistics and reports
  • Verify Premium purchases

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For processing in-app purchases

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
